# Pokémon sprite dataset — NOTICE

`pokemon_16x16_aug.npy` contains 16×16 RGB sprites derived from **Pokémon Gold/Silver
(Game Boy Color)**, extracted from a public sprite sheet and augmented (horizontal
flips + small pixel shifts) into ~2530 samples for this course.
